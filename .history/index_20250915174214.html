No problem! We'll work with you to identify items you want to keep, donate, or dispose of. You have full control over what stays and what goes.

Do you recycle and donate items?

Yes! We responsibly dispose of all items, recycling what we can and donating usable items to local charities whenever possible.

Are there any additional fees?

Our $499 price includes everything for a standard garage cleanout. Additional fees may apply for jobs that require a custom quote (hoarder-level garages, extremely heavy items, or difficult access).

We don't take hazardous materials (paint, chemicals, fuel, solvents), pressurized tanks, or biohazards. Extremely heavy or commercial equipment may require a special quote.
